## Every device on a network is assigned a unique Internet Protocol(ip) address.

**IPv4** \
    : A 32-bit address (e.g., 192.168.1.1).
    
**IPv6** \
    : A 128-bit address designed to replace IPv4 due to the limited number of available addresses.
